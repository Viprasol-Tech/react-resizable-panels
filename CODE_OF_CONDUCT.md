# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge a harassment-free, welcoming, inclusive community. Positive behaviour
includes empathy, respect for differing views, and graceful acceptance of
feedback. Harassment, insults, and publishing others' private information are
unacceptable. Report concerns to support@viprasol.com; all reports are reviewed
promptly and fairly. Adapted from the Contributor Covenant v2.1
(https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
